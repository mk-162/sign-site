# Choosing the Right Sign Material

Not all safety signs are created equal. The material you choose can significantly impact durability, visibility, and compliance.

## Material Options

### Rigid Plastic (1mm)

**Best for:** Indoor applications, temporary signage

- Cost-effective
- Lightweight
- Easy to install
- UV resistant options available

### Self-Adhesive Vinyl

**Best for:** Smooth surfaces, quick installation

- No drilling required
- Professional appearance
- Repositionable options available
- Weather-resistant variants

### Aluminum Composite (3mm)

**Best for:** Outdoor use, high-traffic areas

- Extremely durable
- Weather-proof
- Professional finish
- 10+ year lifespan

### Photoluminescent

**Best for:** Emergency exits, power outage scenarios

- Glows in the dark
- No electricity required
- Essential for emergency routes
- Meets emergency lighting standards

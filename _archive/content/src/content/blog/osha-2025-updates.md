# OSHA 2025 Updates: What You Need to Know

The Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) has announced several key updates to workplace signage requirements that will take effect in 2025.

## Key Changes

### 1. Enhanced Hazard Communication

New requirements mandate more specific hazard symbols for chemical storage areas.

### 2. Photoluminescent Requirements

Emergency exit signs in buildings with more than 50 occupants must now include photoluminescent elements.

### 3. Digital Signage Standards

For the first time, OSHA has issued guidance on the use of digital safety displays.

## Timeline

- **March 2025**: New guidelines published
- **June 2025**: Compliance grace period begins
- **January 2026**: Full enforcement

## How to Prepare

1. Audit your current signage
2. Identify gaps in compliance
3. Order replacement signs early
4. Train your team on new requirements

Stay ahead of the curve by updating your signage now.

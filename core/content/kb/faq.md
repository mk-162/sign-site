# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about safety signage.

## Ordering & Delivery

### How quickly can I receive my order?

Most stock items ship same-day for next-day delivery. Custom signs typically take 3-5 working days.

### Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! Orders of 10+ items receive 10% off, 50+ items receive 20% off. Contact us for larger quantities.

### Can I return signs?

Stock items can be returned within 30 days unused. Custom signs are non-returnable.

## Materials & Durability

### Which material should I choose for outdoor use?

Aluminum composite is best for outdoor use due to its weather resistance and 10+ year lifespan.

### Are your signs UV resistant?

Yes, all our signs use UV-resistant inks and materials to prevent fading.

### How do I clean safety signs?

Use warm water and mild detergent. Avoid abrasive cleaners or solvents.

## Compliance

### Are your signs compliant with current regulations?

All signs comply with **BS EN ISO 7010** and the **UK Health and Safety (Safety Signs and Signals) Regulations 1996**.

### Do you provide compliance certificates?

Yes, compliance documentation is available for all products upon request.

### What if regulations change?

We update our product line promptly when standards change and notify affected customers.

## Custom Signs

### Can you create bespoke signs?

Yes! We offer custom sizing, text, and branding. Contact our team for a quote.

### What's the minimum order for custom signs?

There's no minimum order for custom signs.

### Can I add my company logo?

Absolutely. Provide your logo file and we'll incorporate it into your design.

Color Usage Guidelines

Primary Brand Color: Safety Orange (#FF7300)

  • Use for the word "Hub" in all logo variations
  • Use for accent elements, call-to-action buttons, and highlights
  • Provides high visibility and reinforces safety messaging
  • Never use reversed or at less than 100% opacity

Text Colors

  • Light Mode: Use White (#FFFFFF) for "Safety Sign" text on dark backgrounds
  • Dark Mode: Use Deep Navy (#1A2C43) for "Safety Sign" text on light backgrounds
  • GTSE Brand Tag: Always use Light Grey (#A0A0A0) for subtle brand attribution

Typography

Primary Typeface: Inter ExtraBold

Inter is a modern, highly legible sans-serif typeface designed specifically for digital interfaces. We use Inter ExtraBold (weight: 800) exclusively for all logo and headline applications.

Logo Usage Guidelines

Clear Space Requirements

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "S" in "Safety". This ensures the logo has proper breathing room and maximum visibility.

Minimum Size

Size Requirements

  • Digital/Screen: Minimum 120px wide
  • Print: Minimum 1 inch (2.54cm) wide
  • Always maintain aspect ratio when scaling
  • Never stretch or distort the logo

What NOT to Do

‚ùå Prohibited Uses

  • Do not change the logo colors
  • Do not rotate or skew the logo
  • Do not add effects (shadows, outlines, glows)
  • Do not rearrange or separate logo elements
  • Do not place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Do not recreate or redraw the logo

Background Guidelines

‚úì Use Light Mode When:

  • Background is dark (black, navy, dark grey)
  • Background is a dark photo/image
  • High contrast is needed

‚úì Use Dark Mode When:

  • Background is light (white, cream, light grey)
  • Background is a light photo/image
  • Printing on white paper

‚úì Use With GTSE When:

  • Official brand communication
  • Marketing materials
  • Partnership presentations
  • Legal documents

‚úì Use Without GTSE When:

  • Space is limited
  • Informal communications
  • Social media posts
  • App interfaces
